ABOUT VICTORIA HALL

Victoria Hall was opened in 1st January 2012 as the pioneer hall aimed to be self catered and mixed sex accommodation. Victoria Hall endeavors to provide residents with a home away from home serenity experience. Victoria Hall is conveniently located between Swinburne Sarawak and one-minute walk to Spring Shopping Mall. Victoria Hall is also within the immediacy to RHB Bank and Department of Immigration.
